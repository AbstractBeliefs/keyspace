Welcome to Keyspace

Welcome to Keyspace, an identity and profile proof service.

Keybase allows you to lay claims to your profiles around the web with cryptographic keys. By signing statements saying that you control an account, people can be certain that the account belongs to you.

This means that if someone knows you through a proven microblogging account, they can trust that any other accounts around the web signed by the same key also belong to you, and they can find these all in one place on your Keyspace profile.
